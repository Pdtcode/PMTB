<objective>
Build the LearningLoop that detects trade resolutions via Kalshi settlement polling, feeds resolved outcomes back into XGBoost retraining with recency weighting, triggers retraining on both a periodic schedule (configurable, default weekly) and Brier score degradation, and schedules daily full metric recomputation. Wire the LearningLoop into PipelineOrchestrator so it starts and stops with the application.

Purpose: Closes the feedback loop — the system learns from its mistakes and adapts to market regime changes. Without this, the XGBoost model degrades over time as market dynamics shift. Wiring into the orchestrator ensures the loop actually runs in production.

Output: `src/pmtb/performance/learning_loop.py` with full test coverage, XGBoostPredictor.train() updated to accept sample_weight, and PipelineOrchestrator updated to manage LearningLoop lifecycle.
</objective>

       - `def stop(self) -> None` — scheduler.shutdown(wait=False)
